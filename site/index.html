Current OSE Lab Summer Boot Camps Postponed

The OSE Lab summer boot camps are currently postponed due to COVID-19 restrictions. We are grateful for the generous support of the Charles Koch Foundation and University of Chicago from 2017 to 2019. OSE Lab will be searching for new funding for future boot camps. In the meantime, OSE Lab will continue to support its current research initiatives and student researchers. Thanks to all the students, instructors, and staff who have made the OSE Lab such a stimulating and collaborative network over the last three years. We look forward to continued future developments.

2019 OSE Lab Boot Camp teams up with Econometric Society Dynamic Structural Economics Workshop

We are excited to announce that the 2019 OSE Lab Boot Camp has partnered with the Econometric Society Dynamic Structural Economics Workshop and Conference for our summer boot camps for the next three years. This year, during second week of the OSE Lab Boot Camp, July 8 through July 14, OSE Lab students will join with the other participants in the Dynamic Structural Economics Workshop and the associated two-day conference to learn discrete choice dynamic programming, structural estimation, and dynamic games.
